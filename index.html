FAQ's

How much does a custom website cost in Brisbane?

Most Brisbane small businesses spend anywhere from $1000 to $3,000+ depending on how the site is made, page count, design depth, copywriting, integrations, and how conversion-focused the build is. At The MX Studio: we price simple. $1500 for a 3 page build, then an additional $250 per page. Price per page cost can range from $150 to $350 depending on the perplexity and length of each page. A standard site for a small business may need 7 pages, so you are looking at a minimum of $2500. If you want copywrite, multiple landing pages, and highly (we're talking highly) customized pages then that pricepoint will go up.

What’s included in ongoing website maintenance?

Ongoing maintenance covers security and uptime monitoring, backups, small content updates, performance checks, broken-link fixes, technical SEO hygiene, and support when something needs adjusting. For custom-coded sites like ours, it’s less about plugin updates and more about keeping your site fast, stable, and current as your business grows.

Can you migrate from WordPress without losing rankings?

Yes - if the migration is handled carefully. There will always be a slight dip, but the key is preserving URL structure where possible, mapping 301 redirects for anything that changes, keeping titles and core on-page content aligned, and protecting internal links, canonical tags, sitemap setup, and indexability rules. A clean migration also includes a crawl before and after launch to catch broken links, missing metadata, and orphan pages before they impact traffic.

How long does SEO take to work for Brisbane local searches?

Local SEO momentum usually builds over weeks to months depending on competition, your current site quality, and how consistent your local signals are. The fastest improvements often come from technical fixes, better service-page targeting, and strengthening your Google Business Profile with reviews and consistent information. Sustainable rankings come from authority over time. Solid structure first, then ongoing content and trust signals.

What’s included in an SEO foundation for a small business website?

An SEO foundation is the technical and on-page base that makes your site easy to crawl, understand, and trust. It typically includes clean site architecture and internal linking, semantic headings, metadata, indexing controls, sitemap and robots setup, performance optimization, basic schema, and local relevance signals that match how people search for your services in Brisbane. It’s what sets your site up to rank before you spend time on ongoing SEO.
