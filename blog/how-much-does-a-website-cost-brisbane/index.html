The Real Price Range In Brisbane

The difference comes down to how the site is built, how many pages you need, and whether the focus is purely design or performance and SEO. If you want a build that’s structured to convert and scale, start with our Website Design service and you’ll get a clear baseline for what’s involved.

What Impacts Website Cost?

Custom Build vs Template

Template sites are cheaper upfront but often come with performance limitations and messy structure. Custom-coded builds cost more initially, but they’re faster, cleaner, and built around long-term ranking and conversion. If you’re comparing builders based on visibility and leads, your SEO foundation matters just as much as the visuals.

Page Count

A 3-page brochure site costs significantly less than a 7–10 page conversion-focused build with dedicated service pages. If your goal is to rank for multiple services in Brisbane, you need pages that target those searches properly. That’s exactly why we build structured service pages into our web design builds.

SEO Foundation

A website built properly includes semantic headings, internal linking, metadata, schema, and performance optimisation. Many cheaper builds skip this entirely, which is why businesses end up paying again later. If you want the full picture of what “SEO-ready” actually means, see our SEO services.

Why Cheap Websites Usually Cost More Later

The $800–$1,200 range often means heavy themes, slow load times, and minimal SEO structure. That usually leads to poor rankings, low enquiries, and eventually a rebuild. If you’re unsure whether your current site is costing you leads, start with a proper audit and reach out via our contact page.

What A Performance-Focused Website Includes

A properly built site includes clean architecture, fast load times, mobile optimisation, clear service pages, and internal linking designed to support local visibility in Brisbane. This is the difference between a site that “exists” and a site that generates enquiries. If you want to see what that looks like in practice, our Website Design service outlines what’s included.

And once your site is live, keeping it fast, stable, and updated is what protects your rankings and performance long-term. That’s where ongoing Website Maintenance makes the difference.
