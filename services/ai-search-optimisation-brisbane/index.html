FAQ's

What is AI Search Optimisation (GEO)?

AI Search Optimisation — also called Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO) — is the process of structuring your website and content so AI tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity can understand your business and cite it in their answers. As more people use AI to find local services, showing up in these answers is becoming increasingly important.

Is AI Search Optimisation included in SEO?

Yes. AI Search Optimisation is included as part of both our One-Time SEO Revamp and our Ongoing SEO service. When we do your SEO, we structure your content, schema markup, and site in a way that helps AI tools understand and reference your business — at no extra cost.

How is AI Search different from traditional SEO?

Traditional SEO targets Google's ranking algorithm to appear in organic search results. AI Search Optimisation targets how AI language models understand and summarise your business. The fundamentals overlap — clear content, structured data, and authority — but AI tools also respond to clear FAQ content, direct service descriptions, and how well your site answers specific questions.

Which AI tools does this optimise for?

We optimise primarily for ChatGPT (including its browsing mode), Google's AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity. These are the tools most commonly used for local and business research. The underlying principles — structured content, schema, authority signals — apply broadly across AI search tools.

Do I need to be doing SEO to benefit from AI Search Optimisation?

Because AI Search Optimisation and SEO share the same foundation, we include it as part of our SEO service rather than offering it separately. Starting with a One-Time SEO Revamp is the most effective path to getting seen in both Google and AI search results.
