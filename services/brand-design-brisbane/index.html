FAQ's

How much does brand design cost in Brisbane?

Our brand design packages start from $650 for a logo package through to $3,500+ for a full brand system. The Brand Identity Package — which includes logo, colour palette, typography, and brand guidelines — starts from $1,800. Every project is scoped to what you actually need.

What's included in a brand identity package?

Our Brand Identity Package includes a primary logo and all variations, a curated colour palette, typography rules, and a brand guidelines document. This gives you a consistent system you can apply across your website, print, and social without second-guessing.

Do I need brand design before building a website?

Strongly recommended. A website built without a clear brand identity ends up looking patchy — inconsistent fonts, off-brand colours, a tone that doesn't feel right. Getting the brand right first gives your website design a solid foundation.

How long does brand design take?

Most brand projects wrap in 2 to 4 weeks depending on scope and feedback speed. Logo-only projects move faster. A full brand system with guidelines takes longer. We'll give you a clear timeline when we scope the project together.

Can you redesign an existing brand?

Yes. Whether you need a full rebrand or just a refinement of what you already have, we can work with your existing assets and evolve them — or start fresh if that's the better call. We'll give you an honest recommendation either way.
