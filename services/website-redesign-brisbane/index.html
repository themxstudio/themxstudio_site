FAQ's

How much does website redesign cost in Brisbane?

It depends on what is being kept, what needs changing, how many pages are involved, and whether the current site is worth building on. Some redesigns are focused improvements. Others become full rebuilds.

Can you redesign only part of a site?

Yes, if the current build allows it. Sometimes the right move is refining the core pages or fixing one part of the experience instead of overhauling everything at once.

Will a redesign help with SEO?

It can help a lot if the current structure, speed, internal links, or page hierarchy are getting in the way. A redesign creates a stronger base, and ongoing SEO builds on top of that.

When is a full rebuild smarter than a redesign?

If the codebase fights every change, the layout is too limiting, or the current setup is holding back growth, a full web design project is usually the better investment.

Can you maintain the site after the redesign?

Yes. We can keep the site maintained after launch through website maintenance and pair it with hosting and SEO if you want one team handling the lot.
