FAQ's

What is included in website maintenance?

Website maintenance covers the ongoing work that keeps the site useful: fixes, content updates, small edits, technical tidy-ups, and general support so the site does not slowly fall apart.

Is hosting included with maintenance?

Yes. Hosting is included while we are managing the site through our maintenance plan. That keeps the setup simpler and makes support much easier when something needs attention.

Can you maintain a website you did not build?

In many cases, yes. We first need to look at how the site is built and whether the current setup is stable enough to take over properly. If not, a redesign may be the smarter first move.

Is maintenance included with ongoing SEO?

Yes. If we are doing ongoing SEO, website maintenance can be bundled into that package so the same team is managing both the growth work and the site health.

How quickly do updates or fixes usually happen?

It depends on the job and the urgency, but ongoing clients are not treated like cold one-off work. The point of maintenance is that you already have someone to call when the site needs attention.
