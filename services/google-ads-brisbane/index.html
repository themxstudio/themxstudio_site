FAQ's

How much does Google Ads management cost in Brisbane?

Google Ads management is $650 per month, plus a one-time $650 setup fee. That covers campaign setup, keyword research, ad copy, landing page design and build, monthly reporting, and optimisation. Your ad spend goes directly to Google — we never mark it up.

How is Google Ads different from SEO?

Google Ads puts you at the top of search results immediately through paid placement, while SEO builds organic rankings over time. Ads are ideal for fast results or competitive markets. SEO compounds and keeps working without ongoing spend. The two work best together.

Do I need a new website to run Google Ads?

Not always, but your landing page needs to convert. If someone clicks and lands on a page that's confusing or slow, you've wasted the spend. We build the landing page as part of the campaign, and can handle full website design if a bigger fix is needed.

Are there any lock-in contracts?

No. We don't lock you in with long-term contracts. You can pause or stop at any time. We prefer to keep clients because the results speak for themselves, not because of a contract.

Should I run Google Ads and SEO at the same time?

Yes, if your budget allows for it. Ads get you visible now while SEO builds long-term rankings. Together they cover both ends — immediate traffic and sustainable growth. Many clients start with ads while their SEO gains traction.
