A good logo is simple enough to be instantly recognisable, distinctive enough to stand out, and flexible enough to work in any context. That's harder than it sounds.

Most logos that age poorly were designed for one specific use without thinking about how they'd look as a favicon, in a dark header, or at half the size they were mocked up at. We design with your website's actual use cases in mind from day one.

If you want suburb-specific local pages as well, explore Newstead and Eagle Farm.

A raster logo (like a JPEG or PNG) is made of pixels. Scale it up past its original size and it goes blurry. A vector logo is made of mathematical paths — it scales to any size with zero quality loss, forever.

We design all logos in vector from the start. That means when you need it resized for a new page layout, a different background, or a higher-res export, the file handles it without any quality loss.

A logo is a mark — a visual identifier. Brand design is the full system around it: colour palette, typography, tone of voice, usage rules, and how everything works together across every touchpoint.

A logo alone is a good start. If you need more — colours, typography, and custom graphic elements built for your website — our brand design service covers that. Scoped to what your site actually needs, not a full corporate rebrand.

FAQ's

How much does logo design cost in Brisbane?

Our logo design starts from $650 for a single primary logo, through to $1,800+ for a full logo system with all variations and a basic brand guide. The Logo + Variations package at $950 is the most popular option — it covers your primary logo, a horizontal lockup, stacked version, and icon-only mark.

How many logo concepts will I receive?

We present two initial concepts, refined based on your brief. This keeps the process focused rather than overwhelming you with options that don't fit. Once we land on a direction, you get two rounds of revisions to dial it in.

What file types are included?

Every logo package includes SVG (vector), PNG (transparent background), and PDF files. You'll also receive both full-colour and mono (black and white) versions — so you're covered for any use case, from your website to print to embroidery.

How many revisions do I get?

Every package includes two rounds of revisions. Additional revision rounds can be added if needed. In practice, most clients are happy well before the second round — a solid brief at the start makes a big difference.

Can you redesign my existing logo?

Yes. If your current logo isn't working — too complex, doesn't scale, or just feels dated — we can either refine it or redesign it from scratch. We'll review what you have and give you an honest recommendation on which direction makes more sense.
